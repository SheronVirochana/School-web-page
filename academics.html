Academic Programs

The Royal College has long been renowned for its focus on Academics and all other priorities of the College, be they co-curricular or extra-curricular, are always developed with the focus of supplementing and improving on the academic experience of its students. From its inception in 1978 , the School has increased its student base from 20 to over 4000 with a marginal difference every year according to intake. Each of these students are exposed to the very best of the Sri Lankan education system, with strong infrastructure, a conducive learning environment, and an excellent administrative system to put it all together. This section will explore the Academic infrastructure of the College, starting from entry at different levels to the Curriculum taught in different sections, our Faculty and the achievements of the students who were products of this fine educational institution.
